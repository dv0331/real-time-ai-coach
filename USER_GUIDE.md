## ğŸ“¹ Self-Tape Studio

Professional self-tape recording with analysis.

### Features

- **HD Recording** (720p or 1080p)
- **Framing Guide** - Center yourself perfectly
- **Script Teleprompter** - Paste your sides
- **Take Management** - Save and compare takes
- **Playback Analysis** - Review with AI notes

### How to Record

1. **Navigate to Self-Tape Tab** (top navigation)

2. **Set Up Your Frame**
   - Position yourself within the dotted guide box
   - Your eyes should be at the guide line
   - Leave appropriate headroom

3. **Add Your Script** (right panel)
   - Paste your sides/script into the text area
   - Click "My Lines" to highlight your character's lines

4. **Choose Settings**
   - Quality: 720p (standard) or 1080p (HD)
   - Reader Voice: None, Male, or Female (coming soon)

5. **Record**
   - Click **"Start Recording"**
   - Red REC indicator appears
   - Perform your scene
   - Click **"Stop Recording"**

6. **Review Your Takes**
   - Takes appear in the grid below
   - Click any take to review
   - Use "Analyze Performance" for AI feedback
   - Download or delete as needed

## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Best Results

### Camera Setup

1. **Lighting**
   - Face a window or light source
   - Avoid backlighting
   - Soft, even lighting is best

2. **Framing**
   - Eyes at upper third of frame
   - Leave headroom (not too much)
   - Medium close-up is standard

3. **Background**
   - Neutral, uncluttered
   - Solid color preferred
   - No distracting movements

### Audio

1. **Environment**
   - Quiet room
   - No echo (soft furnishings help)
   - Close doors/windows

2. **Microphone**
   - Built-in webcam mic is fine
   - External mic is better
   - Keep consistent distance

### Performance

1. **Eye Contact**
   - Look at the camera lens, not the screen
   - Imagine it's your scene partner
   - Brief breaks are natural

2. **Pace**
   - Nervous? You're probably rushing
   - Breathe between thoughts
   - Pauses add power

3. **Energy**
   - Project slightly more than feels natural
   - Camera flattens energy by ~20%
   - Be expressive with face and voice

4. **Consistency**
   - Warm up before recording
   - Do multiple takes
   - Compare and learn

## ğŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

### Camera Not Working
- Check browser permissions
- Try a different browser
- Ensure no other app is using camera

### No Audio Detected
- Check microphone permissions
- Speak louder to test
- Check system audio settings

### Metrics Not Updating
- Ensure session is started (Recording status)
- Check WebSocket connection (Ready status)
- Refresh the page

### Face Not Detected
- Improve lighting
- Face the camera directly
- Move closer to camera
